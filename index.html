Top 10 World's Endangered Animals

10

Hawksbill Turtles

Atlantic, the Indian, and the Pacific Ocean

Reason: Accidental capture in fishing gears, nesting habitat degradation, coral reef damage and the illegal trade of hawksbill shells and products.

9

Sumatran Orangutan

Sumatra, Indonesia

Reason: Loss of forest habitat.

8

African Forest Elephant

West and Central Africa

Reason: Poaching, habitat loss due to land-use for agriculture.

7

Black Rhinos

Kenya, Namibia, South Africa and Zimbabwe

Reason: Poaching for their horn.

6

Yangtze Finless Porpoise

Yangtze River, China

Reason: Environmental degradation, overfishing and water pollution.

5

Tapanuli Orangutan

Sumatra, Indonesia

Reason: Loss of habitat due to agriculture, mining, and hydroelectric and geothermal development

4

Mountain Gorillas

Democratic Republic of Congo, Rwanda and Uganda

Reason: Loss of habitat due to the expansion of human settlement.

3

Sunda Island Tiger

Sumatra, Indonesia

Reason: Loss of habitat due to the expansion of human settlemennt and poaching.

2

Amur Leopard

Russia and Nort-Eastern China

Reason: Loss and fragmentation of habitat, prey scarcity and transportation infrastructure such as roads.

1

Javan Rhinos

Java, Indonesia

Reason: Hunting, loss of habitat, invading plant species that leave them with less food. Also, they are extramely vulnerable to extinction due to natural catastrophes, disease, poaching and potential inbreeding.
